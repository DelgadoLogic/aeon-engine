Why Am I Seeing This?

🛡️

Privacy-First Design

Widevine is a Google-owned Content Decryption Module (CDM) that requires a license agreement with Google. It operates as a closed-source binary blob that runs with elevated privileges in your browser. Aeon chooses not to bundle this module to maintain full transparency over every component in the browser.

⚠️

Affected Services

Netflix (HD/4K), Disney+, Amazon Prime Video, Hulu, HBO Max, Spotify (web player), and other streaming platforms that require L1 or L3 Widevine CDM for content decryption. Standard definition (SD) playback via PlayReady may still work on some services.

🔵

PlayReady Supported

Aeon natively supports Microsoft PlayReady DRM through the Windows Media Foundation. Some services (like Netflix on Windows) can negotiate PlayReady instead of Widevine, which may allow up to 4K HDR playback without requiring Google's module.

🔐

Long-Term Solution

DelgadoLogic is actively pursuing a Widevine Master License Agreement (MLA) with Google to enable native DRM support in future Aeon releases — without compromising the browser's privacy architecture or requiring any Google services.
